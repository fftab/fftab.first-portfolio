about me

Florencia Tabarez is a twenty-two-year-old Utah County-based student at the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp. She likes listening to bands like Unknown Mortal Orchestra, Foxygen, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, TV Girl, MGMT and STRFKR while coding. She's vegan, and she loves brufax and sriracha on almost everything. When she's not coding, she's reading about neuroplasticity, nutritional science, counter-economics or solar activity. She has previously lived in south Florida and upstate New York.
